A Trailblazer in Politics

Hon. Hekani Jakhalu Kense stands as a beacon of hope and progress in Nagaland's political landscape. Her journey from grassroots activism to becoming the state's first woman MLA is a testament to her unwavering dedication to public service and social transformation.
